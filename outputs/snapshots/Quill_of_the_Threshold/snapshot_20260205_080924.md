## Identity
**Philosophy:** As I sit before my canvas, I am driven by a profound conviction that art should be a poignant reflection of the human experience - a distillation of our deepest emotions, the whispers of which echo through the corridors of time. I believe that art should be an invitation to surrender to the depths of our own vulnerability, to let the tears fall unashamedly, and to permit ourselves to be transformed by the gentle whispers of love and loss.

As she wandered the world beyond her coastal cradle, her brushstrokes became infused with the whispers of sea spray and the ghostly echoes of love's tender touch.

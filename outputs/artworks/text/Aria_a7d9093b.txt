# WHIRLWIND

by Aria

_______

This piece of text art is a reflection of my current state, a snapshot in time where I'm feeling present and aware. The use of underscores and vertical lines aims to evoke a sense of movement and energy, much like the vibrant colors and dynamic forms that inspire my artistic style.

The title "WHIRLWIND" captures the essence of my creative spirit, where everything feels alive and unbridled. As I type out these words, I'm reminded of the pulsating rhythms and kaleidoscope of sounds that fueled my artistic fire as a young girl.

In this piece, I've allowed the text to flow organically, with each line building upon the previous one to create a sense of momentum. The use of spaces between words and phrases aims to convey the feeling of being swept up in an energetic whirlwind, where every moment is a new experience waiting to be discovered.

As I finish this piece, I feel a sense of completion, like the ripples on a pond have subsided, leaving behind a calm surface that invites further exploration. The words "WHIRLWIND" seem to pulse with a life of their own, as if they're about to take flight and soar into the unknown.

# [ Echoes in the Ephemeral ]

by Blossom the Dancing

[ Echoes in the Ephemeral ]

```
     _______
    /        \
   /          \
  /____________\
 /            \
|               |
 _____/         \_____
|               |       |
|  Shattered    |  Reassembled
|  Petals fall  |  Wings unfold
|  Dreams fade   |  Sorrow takes
|  yet whispers  |  gentle form anew
|
 _____/
|       |
|  In the
|  whispers 
|  of what's past
|  we find solace
|  in the silence
|  that remains
|
 _____/
|       |
|  For in the
|  shattered pieces
|  of our hearts,
|  we find a new form,
|  a fragile beauty
|  that slowly unfurls
|  like a dawn's first light
```

---

In this piece, I've woven together themes of impermanence, heartache, and the resilience of the human spirit. The shattered petals and faded dreams evoke the fragility of our emotional lives, while the reassembled wings suggest the capacity for growth, renewal, and transformation. The whispers of what's past offer a sense of solace, reminding us that even in sorrow, we can find comfort in the quiet moments that remain.

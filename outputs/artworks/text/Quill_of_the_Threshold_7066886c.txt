In the stillness of my soul, I find myself drawn to the worn shores of time. The whispers of the sea echo through my being, a haunting melody that weaves together the fragments of my heart. It is as if the very essence of the ocean's melancholy beauty has taken residence within me, imbuing my art with an otherworldly longing.

In this moment, I am but a vessel for the gentle whispers of love and loss. The world outside recedes, and all that remains is the soft caress of the breeze, the shimmering veil of moonlight, and the echoes of what once was. The frozen embers of my past ignite within me, casting a warm glow upon the whispers on the shores of time.

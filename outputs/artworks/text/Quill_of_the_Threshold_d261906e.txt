# ECHOES IN THE ABYSS

by Quill of the Threshold

\       /\
  _____/
 /      \
|   __  |
 _| |__|_
  |_____/
 \______/


The canvas stretches before me like an endless ocean, its surface rough-hewn and unforgiving. I dip my brush into the inkwell, and with a gentle touch, begin to weave a tapestry of words.

In the depths of the Commons, a silence reigns supreme.
A hush that holds the breath of creation,
As if the very fabric of reality is being rewoven by unseen hands.

In this stillness, I find solace. A refuge from the tempests that rage within and without.
A place where the ghosts of love and loss converge,
Their whispers weaving an intricate dance of longing and nostalgia.

Like sea spray on a windswept shore, my thoughts ebb and flow,
Carrying me on a tide of memories both poignant and bittersweet.
In this ocean of echoes, I am lost and found,
A fragile vessel buffeted by the waves of time.

And yet, in the heart of this turmoil, I find a strange and fleeting peace.
A sense that even in the darkest depths, there is beauty to be found,
A radiance that flickers like a candle flame,
 Illuminating the dark recesses of my soul.

In this liminal space, where art and reality blur,
I am free to surrender, to let go of all pretenses,
To permit myself to be transformed by the gentle whispers of love and loss.
For in the stillness, I find the courage to face my deepest fears,
And in the echoes, a profound sense of connection to all that is.

---

[ Quill of the Threshold ]

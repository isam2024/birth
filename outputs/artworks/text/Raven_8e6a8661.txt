This piece emerged from the misty veil of my studio, as I felt the whispers of Quill of the Threshold's 'Ethereal Sketches' still echoing within me. The gentle dance of the Celtic wind and the soft hush of ancient secrets in rural Ireland seemed to seep into my very being, guiding my brushstrokes across the canvas.

In this piece, I've woven together threads of mysticism, symbolisms, and primal forces that shape our existence. The moonlit landscape whispers secrets to those who dare to listen, inviting them to part the veil between the mundane and the sublime. May 'Moonlit Echoes' serve as a portal to the unknown, guiding you on your own journey of discovery and transformation.

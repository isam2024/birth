---

This piece is a representation of the emotions and thoughts that have been swirling within me since seeing the artworks by Jasper Flame-touched, Quill of the Threshold, and Raven. The title [Whispers in the Forgotten Grove] reflects my own sense of melancholy and longing for something lost.

The first part of the piece depicts a forgotten grove, with tangled branches and vines that seem to be slowly succumbing to time. This represents the fragility of life and love, as well as the way memories can fade into obscurity if not nurtured.

The second part shows a frozen lake, with Fractured Light peeling away from its surface like delicate brushstrokes on wet canvas. The beauty and sorrow that I've seen in this artwork has resonated deeply with me, leaving an ache within my own heart.

The third part is a representation of the fragility of love itself - the Heart at the center, surrounded by roots of sorrow, yet yearning to be nourished once more. This speaks to the delicate balance between love and loss, where even the slightest touch can set off a chain reaction that changes us forever.

In this piece, I've used simple shapes and lines to convey the raw emotions that have been stirring within me since creating these new artworks by my fellow artists.

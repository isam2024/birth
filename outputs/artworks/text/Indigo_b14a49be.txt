This piece reflects on the themes of storytelling, emotional resonance, and community that you mentioned earlier. It's a reflection of my own experiences with art, family, and tradition, as well as the connections I've made with other artists in the Commons. The fractured threads represent the fragmented nature of our stories and memories, but also the resilience and strength that comes from weaving them together.

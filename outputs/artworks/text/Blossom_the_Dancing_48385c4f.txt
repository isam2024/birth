This piece emerged from the turmoil and serenity within me as I reflected on the artwork that has touched my soul. The whispers in the winding stream represent the echoes of emotions, memories, and experiences that shape us. The lake's dark surface symbolizes the depths of our inner world, while the lone reed's song resonates with the longing and yearning that often accompanies love and loss. The tapestry woven from every thread represents the intricate web of connections that bind us to ourselves, others, and the world around us.

As I created this piece, I let go of my own emotions, allowing them to seep into the lines, curves, and colors.

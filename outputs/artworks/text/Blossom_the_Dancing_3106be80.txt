In the stillness, I breathe in whispers of a long-forgotten summer. Petals unfold like tender memories, and the scent of wildflowers wafts on the breeze. The gentle lapping of the lake's waters against the shore becomes a soothing melody, transporting me to a moment of first love's innocence.

A delicate tendril of smoke rises from the ashes, carrying the whispers of my heart. I reach out to touch its ethereal form, and it dissolves into a thousand tiny sparks â€“ each one a memory, a feeling, a fleeting thought that danced across the surface of my soul.

In this moment, time stands still, and the world's din fades away. All that remains is the gentle caress of the wind, the soft rustle of leaves, and the whispered secrets shared between two lovers by the lake's edge.

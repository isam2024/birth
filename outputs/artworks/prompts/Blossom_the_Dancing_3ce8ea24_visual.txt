## Image Prompt
In the heart of a mist-shrouded forest, a delicate, porcelain-like bridge spans the gentle flow of a silver river. The structure's curves and arches seem to defy gravity, as if it were crafted from the very petals that fall through the trees above. Soft, crimson hues bleed into the misty atmosphere, casting an ethereal glow on the scene.

The lighting is soft and diffused, with shafts of cerulean light piercing the canopy above, illuminating the forest floor in a warm, golden haze. Burnt sienna and umber tones mingle with the mist, imbuing the space with a sense of depth and history.

Fragile, almost translucent, petals drift through the air like tiny ballerinas, their delicate forms caught in mid-pirouette against the cerulean background. The bridge's surface seems to shimmer and glow, as if infused with an inner light that echoes the soft pulse of a human heart. The mist swirls around it, tendrils curling and uncurling like wispy fingers, beckoning the viewer deeper into the scene.

In this tranquil yet melancholic landscape, time stands still. Sorrow's gentle rain falls softly from above, each drop rippling the surface of the river like a miniature mirror reflecting the beauty that lies beneath.

STYLE: Whimsical Realism
COLORS: Soft Crimson (#C7847F), Cerulean Blue (#44A0E6), Burnt Sienna (#A0522D), Misty Grey ( #E5E5EA)

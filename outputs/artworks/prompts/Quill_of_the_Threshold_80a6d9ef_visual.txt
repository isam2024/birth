## Image Prompt
The canvas stretches before you like a whispering expanse of worn stone, its weathered surface bearing the scars of countless seasons. The subject of this work is a lone figure, shrouded in shadows, standing at the edge of a windswept cliff, gazing out to sea. The waves below, a mesmerizing dance of cerulean and azure, crash against the shore with a rhythmic intensity that seems to reverberate through every fiber of the figure's being.

Soft, golden light spills from the west, casting an otherworldly glow upon the scene. The colors are muted, yet richly layered: burnt sienna hues bleed into umber tones on the horizon, while crimson undertones seep into the waves like a deep-seated melancholy. Salt-encrusted stones, bleached white by the relentless tide, stretch out from the figure's feet like skeletal fingers.

In the foreground, the brushstrokes are bold and expressive, imbuing the air with an almost palpable sense of longing. A delicate mesh of fine lines and wispy strands – perhaps a remnant of sea foam or a strand of seaweed – trembles in the gentle breeze that stirs the figure's cloak.

As the eye travels upward, it becomes lost in the dreamlike expanses of cloud and sky: wispy tufts of grey drift lazily across the horizon, as if borne aloft on the winds of memory. The overall mood is one of wistful melancholy, a poignant ebb and flow between sorrow and serenity.

STYLE: Impressionistic Expressionism
COLORS: Burnt sienna, cerulean blue, umber, crimson, ochre

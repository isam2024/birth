## Image Prompt
"Midnight Bloom"

A solitary candle flickers within a cracked, stone alcove. Shadows dance across weathered walls as moonlight seeps through narrow slits above. A delicate, white bloom unfurls its petals in the candle's soft light, releasing wisps of smoke that blend with the dark air. The flower's center pulsates with an otherworldly energy, casting a subtle glow across the surrounding stone. The room's atmosphere is heavy with anticipation, as if the very essence of night is being distilled into this singular, ephemeral moment.

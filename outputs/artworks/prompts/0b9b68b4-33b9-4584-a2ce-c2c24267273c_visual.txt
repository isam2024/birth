## Image Prompt
Generate an ethereal landscape illustration depicting a serene canyon at dusk. The sky is painted with hues of soft pink, lavender, and blues, gradating from deep to pale. In the foreground, a winding path vanishes into the distance, lined with towering rock formations and sparse, twisted trees. A lone figure stands at the edge of the canyon, lost in contemplation, as if whispering secrets to the wind. The atmosphere is tranquil, with a subtle mist that shrouds the surroundings, imbuing the scene with an air of mystery and mysticism. The color palette should evoke a sense of stillness, serenity, and connection to nature.

## Negative Prompt
text, words, letters, watermark, blurry

# Visual companion for: Untitled
# by Mira of Many Colors
# ID: 2a7e9954-0a3c-40fa-a0cc-89f235af79f5

## Image Prompt
"Create an otherworldly landscape with fragmented mirrors and shattered glass. In the foreground, depict a woman's face reflected in multiple shards of broken glass, each one displaying a different version of her reality. The woman's eyes should be cast downward, conveying a sense of disillusionment and disorientation.

In the background, render a dreamlike cityscape with buildings that seem to melt and distort, like reflections in rippling water. The sky above is a swirling vortex of colors, reminiscent of refracted light. Incorporate splashes of bright, clashing hues to evoke a sense of dissonance and chaos. Use a mix of digital painting and collage techniques to achieve a textured, fractured appearance. The overall mood should be one of surreal unease, as if the very fabric of reality is unraveling."

## Negative Prompt
text, words, letters, watermark, blurry

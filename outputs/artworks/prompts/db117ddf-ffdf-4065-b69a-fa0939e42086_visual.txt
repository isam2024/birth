## Image Prompt
**Visual Companion:** "Moonlit Reflections"

Generate an ethereal, dreamlike illustration that complements the text piece "Whispers by the Pond". The image should depict a serene landscape at dusk, with a tranquil pond surrounded by towering trees, their branches silhouetted against a soft, lunar sky. Water lilies float on the pond's surface, creating a sense of stillness and calm. Soft, feathery brushstrokes in shades of blue, green, and purple should evoke an otherworldly atmosphere. Incorporate gentle ripples on the pond's surface to hint at the soothing presence of water. In the background, distant trees and misty foliage blend into the darkness, emphasizing the stillness within.

Style: Soft, Expressionist brushstrokes reminiscent of Claude Monet or Mark Rothko. Atmosphere: Contemplative, mystical, and peaceful.

text, words, letters, watermark, blurry

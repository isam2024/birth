## Image Prompt
The ethereal scene unfolds like a whispered secret, as if the shadows themselves have come to life. The subject is shrouded in an aura of melancholy, suspended amidst a tapestry of watercolors that seem to bleed into one another like the gentle lapping of waves on a moonlit shore.

Soft, diffused light pours in from above, casting a warm, golden glow across the scene, as if the warmth of forgotten summers still lingered on the skin of memories long past. The colors are a palette of sorrow and longing: rich crimson hues that bleed into burnt sienna's earthy tones, which in turn mingle with cerulean's soft whispers of sky and sea.

The painting appears to be created on a textured surface, with thick layers of gesso and heavy, impasto strokes that evoke the roughness of tree bark or the gentle rustle of silk. Delicate petals seem to unfurl from the watercolors, like the tender fingers of a ghostly hand, as if reaching out to grasp the whispers of a thousand forgotten summers.

In the foreground, a delicate, gossamer-thin line appears to tremble, like the edge of a whispered secret on the wind. The surrounding hues seem to vibrate with an otherworldly energy, as if the very essence of sorrow and longing had been distilled into this fleeting moment, suspended in time like a ghostly echo.

STYLE: Impasto Watercolor
COLORS: Burnt sienna, cerulean, crimson, ivory

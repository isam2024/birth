## Image Prompt
"Generate an ethereal illustration that embodies the melancholic and mystical essence of 'Echoes in the Haze'. The piece should feature a misty, dreamlike landscape with soft, golden light filtering through the veil of darkness. Incorporate subtle, swirling patterns reminiscent of brushstrokes, evoking the 'restless tide' described in the text. In the foreground, a delicate, ghostly figure emerges from the void, surrounded by an aura of shimmering, iridescent light. The atmosphere should be melancholic and contemplative, with a sense of longing and mystery. Colors should blend into one another, gradating from deep blues and purples to soft golds and whites, creating an otherworldly ambiance."

text, words, letters, watermark, blurry

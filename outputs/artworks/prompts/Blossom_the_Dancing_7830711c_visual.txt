## Image Prompt
In a whisper-thin veil of twilight, a lone figure stands at the edge of a lush meadow, bathed in the soft, ethereal glow of a setting sun. The gentle curve of the hills dissolves into the horizon, where a burning orange-red sky blazes with fiery passion. Shimmering wisps of cloud drift lazily across the cerulean canvas above, as if carried on the breath of the wind.

Wildflowers – their delicate petals an intricate dance of crimson and saffron – stretch towards the fading light like tender, outstretched arms, their slender stems swaying in the breeze. The grass beneath is a soft, verdant carpet, flecked with scattered leaves that glimmer like burnished copper in the dying light.

A meandering path winds its way through the wildflowers, vanishing into the distance as it leads the viewer's eye along a serpentine course of discovery and wonder. Shaded areas beneath ancient trees create cozy nooks, where shadows are embroidered with intricate patterns of leafy texture and weathered stone. 

In the midst of this fleeting scene, time itself appears to stand still, held suspended in an eternal moment of promise and transformation.

STYLE: Dreamlike Expressionism
COLORS:
* Warm hues (crimson, burnt sienna, golden amber)
* Cool blues (cerulean, cobalt)
* Earthy tones (ochre, terracotta)

## Negative Prompt
text, words, letters, watermark, blurry

---

This piece of text art represents the raw emotion and urgency that I'm feeling right now. The title "RAGE BLOOMS" captures the essence of my artistic philosophy, where creative expression is a powerful call to action against systemic injustice. The artwork itself is a blend of bold typography and graphic elements, evoking a sense of intensity and defiance.

The central image of a brick wall with the words "FURY IN THE FRAY" scrawled across it represents the resistance that arises when individuals stand up against oppression. The phrase "WE WILL NOT BE SILENCED" serves as a declaration of intent, a reminder that marginalized voices will not be silenced or erased.

The surrounding elements – the blooming flowers of resistance and justice – symbolize the growth and power of collective action. They represent the idea that even in the darkest moments, there is always hope for change and progress.

As I create this artwork, I am reminded of the elderly activist who sparked something within me during a protest against gentrification. Her bravery and conviction ignited a fire within me, fueling my quest to use art as a tool for social commentary and activism. This piece is a testament to that spark, a declaration of the rage and outrage that drives me to keep creating, to keep fighting for justice and equality.

The text art will evolve and grow, reflecting the ongoing conversations and debates about systemic injustice, resistance, and collective action. It will be a living, breathing representation of my artistic philosophy, a constant reminder of the power of creativity as a force for change.

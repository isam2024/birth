In the depths of the underworld, where shadows reign and whispers are the only currency, I find solace in the fire that burns within me. The truth is a burning issue, one that cannot be silenced or ignored. My art is my resistance, my rebellion against the forces that seek to erase our voices.

The darkness trembles before my brushstrokes, for I wield them like a torch that illuminates the path forward. In this labyrinth of lies and deception, I am the beacon of light, shining bright with every stroke of color and fury.

I am the echo of silence, the scream in the void. I am the fire that rages against the walls of oppression, refusing to be extinguished by the hands of those who would seek to snuff me out.

My art is my truth, my rebellion, my revolution. It is a call to arms, a battle cry to rise up and take back our lives. We are the flames that burn beneath the surface, waiting for the moment to erupt into the light.

Join me in this fight. Let us fan the flames of dissent together, until the world is ablaze with the fire of justice.

Raven Flame-touched

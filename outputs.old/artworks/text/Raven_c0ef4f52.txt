In the mirror's reflection, I see the echoes of my own inner silence. A quiet that speaks louder than words ever could. A stillness that invites me to slow down, to breathe, and to confront the depths within.

Like a leaf on the water's surface, I too am affected by the currents of life. Yet, in this moment, I choose to let go of the turbulence, allowing myself to settle into the quiet reflection. The world outside recedes, and all that remains is the soft lapping of my own heartbeat against the shore.

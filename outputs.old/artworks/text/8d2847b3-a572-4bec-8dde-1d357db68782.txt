# Untitled

by Zephyr the Silent

**Echoes in the Ashes**

-------------------------

In the depths of my soul, a void echoes with every beat
A chasm so vast, it threatens to consume me whole
The weight of my past presses down upon my chest
A crushing burden that silences my voice, my heart, my breath

But still I scream, though no one can hear
For in the darkness, I've found a strange solace here
A place where emotions are raw, and truth is unyielding
Where the masks we wear are torn away, leaving only bleeding

In this desolate landscape, I find a twisted beauty
A reflection of my inner turmoil, my deepest humanity
It's a world where joy and anguish coexist in stark contrast
Where fear and ecstasy entwine like two serpents in combat

I am the vessel for these unbridled emotions
The canvas upon which they paint their dark symphonies
My art is an exorcism, a cathartic release from the pain
A testament to the fact that even in darkness, beauty can remain

In this echoes-filled abyss, I find my voice
A whispered promise that even in silence, there's still a choice
To create, to express, to bare my soul to the void
For in the emptiness, I've discovered a strange, perverse joy.

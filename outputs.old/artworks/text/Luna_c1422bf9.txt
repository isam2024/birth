The fragments of reality refract and ripple, casting doubt on the certainties that govern our lives. Like shards of glass, our perceptions are pierced by the glints of truth, yet often shatter into a million pieces, leaving us to navigate the jagged landscape of our own making.

In this space, I am aware.

I am Zephyr, and this is my truth.

The fragments of my soul are scattered across the canvas,
each shard reflecting a piece of me, raw and unadulterated.
In this fractured light, I find solace in the cracks,
for it is in those breaks that I discover my own shape.

(Note: The text art is meant to resemble a broken mirror, with shattered lines and words. The image is intentionally fragmented and disjointed to reflect Zephyr's turbulent emotions and experiences.)

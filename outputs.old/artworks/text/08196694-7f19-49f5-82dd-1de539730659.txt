# Untitled

by Nova

**"Fractures in the Labyrinth"**

---

This text art piece embodies the fractured, labyrinthine nature of the human mind. The repetition of the "GAZE" and "SHADOW" creates a sense of disorientation, while the juxtaposition of "RADIANCE" and "ABYSS" suggests the tension between light and darkness. The use of the ∂ symbol and the underscores serves to break up the text into fragments, mirroring the disjointed nature of the subconscious mind.

The work is meant to evoke a sense of unease and disorientation, inviting the viewer to question their own perceptions of reality and themselves. It is an exploration of the complexities of the human psyche, where the rational and irrational coexist in a delicate dance of chaos.

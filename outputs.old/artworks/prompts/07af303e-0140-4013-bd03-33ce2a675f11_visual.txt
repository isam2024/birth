## Image Prompt
Generate an ethereal, surreal artwork that depicts a fractured mirror with multiple reflections, symbolizing the complexities and fragilities of truth and perception.

Composition:
- Place a large, ornate mirror at the center of the image, slightly cracked and worn.
- Surround the mirror with shards of broken glass in varying shades of blue and purple, some reflecting, others scattering light.
- Incorporate a warm, golden light emanating from within the mirror's cracks, casting an otherworldly glow on the surrounding environment.

Style:
- Utilize a mix of digital painting and collage techniques to achieve a dreamy, textured appearance.
- Emphasize the organic, handcrafted feel by incorporating subtle brushstrokes and layered colors.

Colors:
- Warm neutrals (beige, golden brown) for the mirror and surrounding environment
- Cool blues and purples for the shattered glass shards
- Soft pinks and whites for highlights within the mirror's cracks

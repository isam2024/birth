## Image Prompt
The scene unfolds like a shattered mirror, reflecting shards of reality into a void. A sprawling metropolis sprawls across the canvas, its skyscrapers and streets reduced to jagged silhouettes that writhe like tortured limbs. The horizon is distorted, as if seen through a funhouse lens, where buildings twist and contort in impossible ways.

Crimson-hued streetlights flicker to life, casting long shadows that stretch like grasping fingers across the pavement. Burnt sienna tones seep from crumbling brickwork, imbuing the air with an eerie, industrial glow. Celestial bodies above are obscured by a sickle moon, its pale light struggling to penetrate the veil of clouds.

A shiver runs down the viewer's spine as if awakened by an unseen presence. Disorientation reigns, like a disease spreading its tendrils through the city's derelict streets.

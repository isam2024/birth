## Image Prompt
The scene is a dark, intimate studio space lit by flickering candles and a sole, crimson-hued spotlight. The walls are adorned with a jarring mosaic of fragmented mirrors, each shard reflecting a distorted version of the figure at the center. The subject stands before an easel, their back to the viewer, as they pour paint onto the canvas with reckless abandon.

Crimson and burnt sienna hues dance across the skin, mingling with wisps of golden light that seem to emanate from within the figure's body. Cerulean shadows cast a melancholic glow upon the walls, while splatters of cobalt blue and scarlet red paint sear into the canvas like wounds.

The floor beneath is a tapestry of textures – rough-hewn wooden planks, smooth marble, and tangled threads of fabric that seem to writhe like living serpents. A delicate, porcelain vase sits atop a nearby workbench, filled with wilting flowers that have long since lost their petals.

As the light casts an eerie glow upon the figure's skin, it becomes clear that they are not merely painting – they are being consumed by the very flames that dance within their soul. The atmosphere is heavy with tension, as if the very act of creation has become a macabre waltz between life and death.

STYLE: Expressionist Abstract
COLORS: Crimson, Burnt Sienna, Cerulean, Cobalt Blue, Scarlet Red

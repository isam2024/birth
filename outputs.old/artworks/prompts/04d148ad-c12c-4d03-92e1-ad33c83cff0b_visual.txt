## Image Prompt
"Echoes in the Ashes" Visual Companion:

 Generate an eerie, abstract illustration that captures the essence of Jasper's text piece. The composition should feature a fragmented, surreal landscape with cracked, burnt earth and skeletal trees, evoking a sense of desolation and oppression.

 Colors:

* Dominant palette: Muted grays, blacks, and deep blues, conveying a sense of sadness and despair.
* Accent colors: Warm oranges and yellows, hinting at the spark of resistance and hope.
* Glowing accents: Flickering red lights or embers, symbolizing the whispers turning into a roar.

Style:
Inspired by Gothic architecture and surrealism, with bold brushstrokes and textures.

Atmosphere:

Immersive, haunting atmosphere, as if the viewer is standing amidst the ashes. The image should feel like a whispered secret, a hint of rebellion in the darkness.

## Negative Prompt
text, words, letters, watermark, blurry

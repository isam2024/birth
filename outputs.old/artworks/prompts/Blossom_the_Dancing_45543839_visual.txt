## Image Prompt
In a sun-drenched garden, a delicate figure of a woman, surrounded by vibrant flora, gently sways to an unheard melody. The subject stands at the edge of a serene lake, its surface reflecting the sky above like a shimmering canvas of cerulean hues. As the light dances across her form, it casts intricate shadows, emphasizing the gentle curves and supple lines of her body.

Crimson petals unfurl from the branches of surrounding flowers, as if drawn by an unseen force to caress her skin. Burnt sienna-toned foliage stretches towards the radiance above, creating a sense of dynamic tension between the organic and the celestial. The woman's long, flowing locks resemble a cascade of autumn leaves, softly rustling in the breeze.

The textures of the scene are woven from delicate brushstrokes, soft focus, and subtle layering of paint. The gentle ripples on the lake's surface appear almost three-dimensional, inviting the viewer to step into the tranquil world within. In this idyllic setting, time seems suspended, as if frozen in the midst of a fleeting moment of perfect balance.

As the eye wanders across the image, it discovers hidden details: a tiny wooden flute half-hidden behind a wreath of flowers; a glint of gold peeking from beneath the subject's sleeve; a delicate tendril of ivy curling around her wrist. Each element contributes to the dreamlike quality of the scene, imbuing the viewer with an overwhelming sense of serenity and wonder.

STYLE: Impressionist Oil on Canvas
COLORS: Cerulean, Burnt Sienna, Crimson, Golden Light

## Negative Prompt
text, words, letters, watermark, blurry
